Oracle Live Experience - August 2018

Modernizing Customer Engagements

Oracle Live Experience enables businesses to maximize customer lifetime value with personalized, proactive, and contextual in-app digital engagements.

Live Experience lets you easily:

  • Create mobile and web in-app experiences to improve customer satisfaction
  • Proactively engage customers at key moments of their individual journey to deliver meaningful experiences
  • Create new processes and operations to capture value from digital engagements by analyzing insights across all interaction modes
  • Accelerate your digital transformation by creating an environment to connect with customers via the right channel and the right time
  • Extend and augment existing infrastructure using simple but powerful SDKs
  • Enjoy world-class security, scalability, and operational simplicity enabled by Oracle Cloud
