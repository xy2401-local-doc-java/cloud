4 Adding the DocuSign Adapter Connection to an Integration

When you drag the DocuSign Adapter into the invoke area of an integration, the Cloud Endpoint Configuration Wizard appears. This wizard guides you through configuration of DocuSign Adapter endpoint properties.

The following sections describe the wizard pages that guide you through configuration of the DocuSign Adapter as an invoke in an integration. The DocuSign Adapter cannot be used as a trigger in an integration.
