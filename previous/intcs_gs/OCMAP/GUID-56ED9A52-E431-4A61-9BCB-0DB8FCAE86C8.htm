Creating Mappings

You can map fields directly from the source data structure to the target data structure in the mapper.

To create mappings:
  1. In the middle of the integration, click the Mapper icon for the request, response, or fault map to edit.
  2. Click Create.

    Description of the illustration GUID-98B18DF4-6C25-4B02-94F0-524D6693E630-default.png

  3. To map fields directly, perform one of the following steps:
    1. Click a field in the source and drag it to the corresponding field in the target.
    2. Click the source and the target fields, and then click Map+.

      Description of the illustration GUID-E84D99B8-6057-4671-93D1-9EA5BEAFF6C9-default.png

      The name of the source field appears in the target Mapping column, and a green check mark icon appears next to both fields. The most recently mapped fields are connected by a green line. Click the green check mark of other sources and targets to see their current mappings. You cannot drag and drop onto a target that is already mapped.

      Description of the illustration GUID-83D3452A-23EE-43B4-BD2D-819D5A41A1B3-default.png

      Note:

      In the mapping summary, the full names of extended elements are not displayed.
