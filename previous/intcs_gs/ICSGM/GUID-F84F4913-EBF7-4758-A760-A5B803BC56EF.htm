4 Adding the Gmail Adapter Connection to an Integration

When you drag the Gmail Adapter into the invoke area of an integration, the Adapter Endpoint Configuration Wizard is invoked. This wizard guides you through configuration of Gmail Adapter endpoint properties.

The following sections describe the wizard pages that guide you through configuration of the Gmail Adapter as an invoke in an integration. The Gmail Adapter cannot be used as a trigger in an integration.
