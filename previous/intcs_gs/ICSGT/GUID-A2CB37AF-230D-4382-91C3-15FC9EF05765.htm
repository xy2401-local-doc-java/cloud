4 Adding the Google Task Adapter Connection to an Integration

When you drag the Google Task Adapter into the invoke area of an integration, the Adapter Endpoint Configuration Wizard is invoked. This wizard guides you through configuration of Google Task Adapter endpoint properties.

The following sections describe the wizard pages that guide you through configuration of the Google Task Adapter as an invoke in an integration. The Google Task Adapter cannot be used as an invoke in an integration.
