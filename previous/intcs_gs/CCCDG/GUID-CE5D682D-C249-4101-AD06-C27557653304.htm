JDeveloper Wizard Design Recommendations

This section lists wizard design recommendations for running an adapter in JDeveloper.

Rule

Justification

A single invocation of an adapter wizard should generate one integration WSDL file and one JCA file.

Under SOA (and OSB) in Jdeveloper, only a single composite service or reference is generated.

Don’t rely on a Textbox or Textarea’s LostFocus event.

In JDeveloper and Swing, the lost focus event is not fired until the focus is on another modifiable field. The wizard user can’t just click on an empty spot on a panel.

Artifact location fields, such as the WSDL location, must supply a Browse button to invoke the standard brower or explorer dialog for finding the artifact.

In JDeveloper, the artifacts, including the enterprise WSDL files, can come from several possible sources. The standard browser or explorer provides access to these sources.

Do not assume that the connection and connection properties are available during wizard initialization or on the Welcome page.

In JDeveloper and the OSB console, no connection is established until the wizard user has navigated from the connection page, which is the second page of most wizards.
