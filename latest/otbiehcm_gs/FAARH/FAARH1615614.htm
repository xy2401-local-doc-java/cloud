About this Guide

This guide explains how to view and work with the delivered analytics in Oracle Transactional Business Intelligence Enterprise for HCM Cloud Service. It is intended for these HCM users who use predefined existing analytics to assist them in strategically managing their workforce:

  • Vice Presidents of Human Resources

  • Human Resource Managers

  • Line Managers

Related Documents and Training

Review videos, tutorials, and other help about HCM analytics in Oracle Transactional Business Intelligence Enterprise and reports in the Oracle Cloud Documentation Library.
