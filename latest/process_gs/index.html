Oracle Process Cloud Service

Get Started

Oracle Process Cloud Service enables you to rapidly design, automate, and manage business processes in the cloud. The service has two environments:

  • Design-Time: for developing, testing, and activating process applications
  • Runtime: for running, monitoring, and managing activated applications
