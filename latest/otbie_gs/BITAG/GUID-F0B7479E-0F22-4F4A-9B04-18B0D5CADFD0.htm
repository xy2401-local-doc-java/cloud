1 Ordering, Activating, and Setting Up Security

You purchase an Oracle Transactional Business Intelligence Enterprise (OTBI Enterprise) service by contacting an Oracle sales representative. When you purchase OTBI Enterprise, you specify the name and details of the person who will be the account activator. The account activator then assigns an account administrator during activation.
