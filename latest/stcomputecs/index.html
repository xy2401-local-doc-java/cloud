Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Compute Classic

Get Started

Easily provision and manage virtual machines and the associated storage and networking resources in Oracle Cloud.

When you subscribe to Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Compute Classic, you can opt for either capacity in a dedicated zone that gives you complete isolation from other tenants, or capacity in a zone that's shared with other tenants.
