How do I share a document with an application?

  1. Open the Share dialog in one of the following ways:

    • While viewing a list of documents, tap under the document.

    • While viewing a message with a document, tap under the message, then tap Share.

    • While viewing the document, tap in the action bar to display the actions menu, then tap Share.

    A list of applications appears.

  2. Tap the application you want to copy the document to.

  3. Tap Always if the application is the one you will always use; tap Just Once, to choose an application each time you share a document.

Alternately, you can share the document while viewing it:

  1. Open the document.
  2. Tap in the action bar to display the actions menu, then tap Share.
