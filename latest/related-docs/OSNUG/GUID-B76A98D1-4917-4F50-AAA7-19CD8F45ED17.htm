How do I join or leave a file co-browsing session?

  1. Open the document.
  2. Above the file, tap .

    A list of the people looking at the file appears.

  3. Tap the person whose movement you want to follow.

To stop following the other person's movement through a file, open the co-browsing menu, and tap the name of the person you're browsing with.
