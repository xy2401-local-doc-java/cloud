How do I manage flags assigned to me?

You can see all the flags assigned to you on the Flags page—from the navigation menu on the left, select Flags. From there you can view the flagged message (and document, if there is one), reply to the message, and clear your flag.

To clear a flag assigned to you, click the flag icon next to the message, and then click next to your name (at the top of the flag pop-up).

Note: When you clear a flag, the person who set the flag might get an email, depending on what they've set up through their notifications.
