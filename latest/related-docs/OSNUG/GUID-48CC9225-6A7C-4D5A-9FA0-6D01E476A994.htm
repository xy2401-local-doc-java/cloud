How do I add a related item to a Social Object?

  1. Open the Social Object.

  2. On the segment bar, tap Related.

  3. In the bottom bar, tap .

  4. Tap the Conversation, user wall, group wall, or Social Object you want to add.

Alternatively, you can create a new Conversation or collection.

  1. Tap .
  2. Tap New Collection to create a collection or New Conversation to create a Conversation.
  3. Enter a name.
  4. Tap Create.
