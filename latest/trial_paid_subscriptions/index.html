Manage Oracle Cloud Accounts and Services

Set up and configure your Oracle Cloud Account so you can manage services, add users, and configure access to your Oracle Cloud resources.

Get Started

Get an Account

Your first step is to get an Oracle Cloud Account by signing up for a free trial, free credits, or a subscription.

Manage Users and Access to Cloud Services

After you activate your Oracle Cloud account, you can add additional users and use several Oracle Cloud options to control access to your account and services.

Manage Your Account and Services

After you configure your account and get familiar with your account, learn how to manage things day-to-day and how to add and manage the Oracle Cloud services required for your organization.
