Adaptive Intelligent Apps for Manufacturing User's Guide
Describes how Oracle Adaptive Intelligent Apps for Manufacturing applies statistical methods to identify hidden patterns and relationships among manufacturing steps and inputs. Models created based on historical data can optimize the factors that prove to have the greatest effect on yield and quality, resulting in improved product performance, decreased scrap and rework, optimized testing, and overall operational efficiency. Comparing real time data collected to these models enables operators to make adjustments to the inputs as necessary to optimize yield and quality. Learn how to create models that optimize any step or operation in a workflow or routing by basing the model on historical data from previous steps in the routing.
