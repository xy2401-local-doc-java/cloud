pcmOpSubscriptionServiceBalgrpTransfer

Componentfm_subscription
Visibilitypublic
Transactionrequired

Description

pcmOpSubscriptionServiceBalgrpTransfer operation is used to transfer service from one balgrp to another balgrp or repoint a balgrp from one billinfo to another billinfo. All services that are being transferred should belong to same bal_grp and can be transferred to one bal_grp only. If the transfer of service is across accounts, only one service/subscription service can be transferred and the target bal_grp has to be created.
