Preface

This guide describes how to use Oracle Monetization Cloud.

Audience

This document is intended for:

  • Business analysts who plan the Oracle Monetization Cloud implementation

  • Operations supervisors and personnel who run billing and collect payments

  • Pricing analysts who design product offerings
