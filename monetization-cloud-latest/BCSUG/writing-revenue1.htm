Writing off an account

A write-off removes revenue that your company has determined a customer will never pay. To write off revenue, you write off the entire account.

Use Subscriber Management to write off an account. Open the account and select Actions, Accounts Receivables, and then Write-Off Account.

You can specify to separate the tax amount from the written off amount.
