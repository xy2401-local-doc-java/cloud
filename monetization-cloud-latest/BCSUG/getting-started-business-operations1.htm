7 Getting started with Business Operations

Use Business Operations to perform basic business operation tasks such as generating invoices for customers, processing batches of payments from your banks and payment processors, and fixing payments that couldn't be processed. Business Operations is an application in Oracle Monetization Cloud.

Important:

  • Do not open multiple instances of Business Operations.

  • To avoid losing data, do not use browser commands such as Back, Forward, and Refresh.

  • Ensure that cookies are enabled in your browser.
