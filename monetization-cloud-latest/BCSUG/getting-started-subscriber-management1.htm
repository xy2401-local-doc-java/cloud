6 Getting started with Subscriber Management

Use Subscriber Management for creating and managing accounts, and for managing billing, payments, and accounts receivable for customer accounts. Subscriber Management is an application in Oracle Monetization Cloud.

Important:

  • Do not open multiple instances of Subscriber Management.

  • To avoid losing data, do not use browser commands such as Back, Forward, and Refresh.

  • Ensure that cookies are enabled in your browser.
